Wiseguys Pizzeria and Pub

Specialty Items

Take a look at our gallery containing pictures and descriptions of some of our best specialty items. Wiseguys would love to show you what we can bring to the table!

Wiseguys has been serving up Appletons's best wood-fired pizzas, pastas and salads since we opened our first location in 1992. We are locally owned and operated. Wiseguys specializes in gourmet pizzas that are hand tossed, then baked in a wood-burning oven for that added flavor. The pastas are made fresh daily and all the sauces and recipes are made on location bringing you that authentic italian cuisine.

Order To-Go!

Here at Wiseguys, every dish is created using only the freshest, finest ingredients. We pride ourselves in creating delicious gourmet quality, yet affordable, pizza's for you and your family. We are proud of our long tradition of serving Appletons's pizza lovers for more than 25 years.
